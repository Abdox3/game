🎯 Objective

Reduce your opponent's Hero HP to 0. Each Hero starts with 100 HP. Deploy creature cards to your 5 lanes, defend your Hero, and break through the enemy's line to deal direct damage.

🃏 Deck Building

  • Build a deck of 12 cards: 8–10 creatures + 2–4 spells
  • Max 2 copies of any single card
  • Must include exactly 1 Forge Commander (Hero card)
  • Running 3+ cards of the same faction activates a Faction Synergy Bonus

⚔️ Combat Rules

  • Each round lasts 2 seconds (wall clock)
  • Creatures attack the opposing card in the same lane (slot)
  • If the opposing slot is empty, attacks redirect to the nearest occupied slot
  • If the entire opposing board is empty, creatures attack the enemy Hero directly
  • The Hero is protected as long as at least one creature is on the field

⭐ Card Rarities

  • ■ Common — 1 skill, low stats, starter constructs
  • ■ Uncommon — 2 skills, utility units
  • ■ Rare — 2 skills, high potential core units
  • ■ Epic — 3 skills, game-changing impact
  • ■ Legendary — 3 skills, faction-defining powerhouses

🌀 Counter Triangle

  • Ember Burn → countered by Ironclad shields + Verdant regen
  • Ironclad Walls → countered by Phantom displacement + Al-Noor pierce
  • Verdant Sustain → countered by Ember anti-heal burn
  • Phantom Stealth → countered by Al-Noor reveal + AoE
  • Al-Noor Synergy → countered by Ember burst before setup
